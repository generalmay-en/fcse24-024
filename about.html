About Ofentse Cooks

Our story, values, and the team behind Botswana's premier catering service

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Ofentse Gumede, Ofentse Cooks began as a small home-based catering service in Gaborone. What started as a passion for creating beautiful, flavorful dishes quickly grew into Botswana's most sought-after catering company.

Drawing inspiration from traditional Botswana cuisine and combining it with modern culinary techniques, we've created a unique dining experience that honors our heritage while embracing contemporary tastes.

Today, we cater to events across Botswana, from intimate family gatherings to large corporate functions, always maintaining our commitment to quality, authenticity, and exceptional service.

Our Mission & Values

Local Ingredients

We source fresh, seasonal ingredients from local farmers and producers across Botswana.

We're committed to supporting and uplifting our local communities through employment and training.

Culinary Excellence

Every dish is prepared with meticulous attention to detail and presentation.

Client Satisfaction

Your event is our priority - we go above and beyond to ensure your complete satisfaction.
